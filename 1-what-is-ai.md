## Defining AI

- AI as "machines performing tasks that would typically require human intelligence."
- Brief, accessible history: rule-based systems → statistical approaches → modern machine learning.
- Clarify common misconceptions: AI is not sentient, not magic, and not one single thing.
- Introduce the distinction between *narrow AI* (task-specific, what exists today) and *general AI* (hypothetical, human-level reasoning).
